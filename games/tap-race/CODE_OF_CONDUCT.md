# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, specific, and constructive.

This game repo welcomes humans and AI-assisted contributors. The human submitting a contribution is responsible for its quality, provenance, and behavior.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, hate speech, personal attacks, spam, and knowingly submitting work that violates third-party rights.

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove contributions and discussions that make the project harder or less safe to collaborate on.
